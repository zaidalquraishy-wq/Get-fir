⚡ Simple Progression Rule
When you hit the top of the rep range on every set, increase the weight next session.
Example: W1 bench → 10, 9, 8 reps. Next session try 10, 10, 9. Once you hit 12, 12, 12 → move the weight up.

Rest times: 60–90 sec on most sets · 90–120 sec on heavier presses, rows, squats & RDLs.
